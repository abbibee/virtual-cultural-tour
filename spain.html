Spain

Welcome to the Spain cultural tour. Here you will find immersive experiences of globally iconic landmarks as well as local culture.

3D Tour: Basílica de Santa María

The Basílica de Santa María in Alicante, Spain, dates back to the 14th century and features a Valencian Gothic style of architecture and ornate Baroque elements. It is the oldest active church in the city. This tour will take you through the interiors of the basilica.

Photo Gallery: Park Güell

Park Güell features several colorful parks and gardens in Barcelona. The UNESCO World Heritage Site has become an icon of the local culture with its beautiful views of the city. Famed architect Antoni Gaudi combined nature and whimsy to create the beautiful park.

3D Tour: European Museum of Modern Art

The European Museum of Modern Art in Barcelona is a museum in an 18th century palace that showcases paintings and sculptures created between the 20th and 21st centuries. This tour will take you through an exhibit of the artworks of Polish sculptor Grzegorz Gwiazda.

Video: Spain Walking Tour

This video by HP Walking Tours shares a captioned walking tour of the city of Barcelona, passing through many iconic locations such as the Gothic Quarter, Gaudí House Museum, Hypostyle Room, and Sagrada Família.
